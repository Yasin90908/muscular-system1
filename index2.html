Organs and their functions

Skeletal Mucles

Function: Responsible for body movements, posture, and heat production. Ex: Biceps Brachii:Helps in bending the elbow. Triceps Brachiii: Helps in straightening the elbow. Quadriceps: Helps in extending the knee. Hamstrings: Helps in bending the knee.

Cardiac

Functions :of Cardiac Muscle Pumps Blood throughout the body. Maintains Heartbeat through automatic contractions. Regulates Blood Flow by adjusting contraction strength. Adapts Heart Rate based on the body's oxygen needs.

Smooth Mucles

Function: : Found in internal organs and helps in involuntary movements like digestion and blood circulation. Ex: Stomach Muscles: Help in digestion by churning food. Intestinal Muscles: Move food through the digestive tract. Blood Vessel Muscles: Control blood pressure by contracting and relaxing
