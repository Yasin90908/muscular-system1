Muscular System

Why is Muscular System important ?

The muscular system is a complex network of muscles vital to the human body. Muscles play a part in everything you do. They:
1.control your heartbeat and breathing
2. help digestion
3. allow movement
The muscles of your body are also responsible for your posture, balance, and coordination.
